6. My opinion on fundamental analysis and technical analysis.

Yes I use both types in my financial analysis. I would prefer fundamental analysis as I believe those fundamental motivations are the force of market. However, the reason of applying technical analysis is that we do not have enough and latest information as the major funds. Hence, as an individual investor, we have to follow the movement of big funds who can make and change the market trends. Technical analysis provides a method to figure out the potential footprints of major funds. We then rely on technical analysis to catch the influence of unknown information.
